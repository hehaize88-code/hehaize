How to review this category

Record scale, quantity and shape before planning the parcel.

01Dimensions, edition and quantity02Shape, seams and included pieces03Compression versus display condition

Collectible figures, plush items and blind-box products are difficult to judge from a cover image because scale and quantity may be unclear. Use the eight local records to keep source IDs and reference prices distinct, then confirm the exact character, edition, dimensions, quantity and box option on the live listing. A series image does not prove that every pictured design is included or selectable.

For QC, request a straight full-item view before zooming into one seam, label, face detail or accessory that would change the decision. Count every promised piece and compare the visible design with the saved option. Soft collectibles may be light but occupy substantial space, while boxed figures need corner protection. Compression may reduce volume, but it can permanently distort filling, faces, display shape or packaging condition.
