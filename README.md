# Hubbuy Sheet

[Hubbuy Sheet](https://hubbuy-sheet.com/) is an independent English-language research and product-discovery website for people comparing China shopping finds and planning warehouse orders. It combines a compact product directory with fact-checked guidance on source verification, quality-control photos, parcel weight, consolidation, and international shipping decisions.

The published website is maintained in the [`hubbuy-sheet-com`](./hubbuy-sheet-com/) directory.

## Editorial approach

Platform statements are checked against publicly accessible Hubbuy pages and dated in the relevant guide. Editorial examples are labeled as examples, and changing details such as fees, routes, delivery estimates, and optional-service availability are not presented as permanent facts. Readers are directed to verify live transaction details before ordering or shipping.

## Independence and relationship disclosure

Hubbuy Sheet is independent from Hubbuy and is not an official Hubbuy website. Product and catalog links may lead to CNBuy Sheet at `cnbuycha.com`, in which this website has a commercial interest. Hubbuy Sheet does not process orders, payments, warehouse services, or international delivery.

## Technical notes

The site is a statically generated Next.js project with localized English, Brazilian Portuguese, and German routes. It includes canonical URLs, structured data, robots directives, and a generated XML sitemap.
